About us

"Word about Barrow's voice is getting around. It’s the type of voice that demands attention."
- ReachProvo.com

Picture leaving your town and traveling the back roads of North America. Picture the sights you'd see, the people you'd meet. Do you miss home? Do you feel small compared to the vastness of the world? Set those feelings to music, and your result will sound a lot like Michael Barrow and The Tourists.

A folk rock act from Provo, Utah, they blend the sound of The Paper Kites with electric guitar tones that would feel at home on John Mayer’s Continuum. The band has developed a reputation for their solid live performance and expansive dynamic range. Producer Brian Zieske (The Hush Sound, The Academy Is…) worked carefully to capture that same live energy on their first record. Their debut album, Juneau, is set for an April, 2017 release. The band is made up of Michael Barrow (Vocals, Guitar), Trevor Harmon (Lead Guitar), Reed Perkins (Drums), Alessandro Improta (Bass), and Zach Collier (Keys).
